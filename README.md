Dashboards to analyze different types of business problems

## Types of Dashboards

- Marketin Campaign

- Sales Performance

- HR

- Logistics

- Financial

- Balance Sheet

- Stock Market

- Detection of Anomalies in Financial Transactions

- Forecasting Industrial Production
